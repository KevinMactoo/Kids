Aboutkidstech

iEARN(International Education and Resource Network) is an organization in more than 140 countries. iEARN empowers educators and young people to work together online using the Internet and other new technologies.
Over 2,000,000 young people each day are engaged in collaborative project work worldwide.

OUR Features

  • What we do

Certificates

We give certificate after completion that show proficiency of the participants in certain knowledge, skills and abilities. Our goal is to motivate and inspire participants to be creative.

Technology Bootcamp

The Technology Bootcamps are practical workshops on chosen topics addressing existing skill gaps. They are designed to motivate, ignite and inspire the participants and to bring their creative ideas to life.

Code club

At Code Club, we think all children should have the opportunity to learn to code, no matter who they are or where they come from. We work with a global community of volunteers, educators, and partners to run free coding clubs where 9 to 13-year-olds build and share their ideas.
